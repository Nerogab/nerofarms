Our Story

Growing Nigeria's Agricultural Future

Nerogab Farms was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between hardworking Nigerian farmers and the consumers and businesses that need their produce. Operating from Ikpoba Hill, Benin City, Edo State, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and fairness.

We supply premium staple crops including rice, maize, cassava, yam, and beans — sourced directly from verified farms across Edo, Delta, Ondo, Kogi, and other agricultural belts of Nigeria.

Our customers range from individual households to restaurants, supermarkets, food processing companies, and NGOs. We offer both retail and bulk supply, with flexible delivery arrangements nationwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Nerogab Farms.

How do I place an order?
+
Browse our Products page, select your items and quantity, then click "Order Now." Fill in your delivery details, get your Order ID, and complete the bank transfer payment using the Order ID as your reference.
What payment methods are accepted?
+
We currently accept bank transfer only. Payment details: Kuda Bank — Account Name: Nerogab Global Services — Account Number: 3002377107. Use your Order ID as the payment reference to ensure your payment is matched correctly.
How long does delivery take?
+
Delivery typically takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. Benin City and South-South region orders are usually fulfilled in 1–3 days. Orders to Lagos, Abuja, and other major cities take 2–4 days. Remote locations may take 4–7 days.
What is the minimum order quantity?
+
There is no strict minimum order for retail customers. However, delivery is more economical for orders of at least ₦20,000. For bulk orders (1 tonne or more), please contact us directly for special pricing and logistics arrangements.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
+
You can cancel or modify your order before payment is confirmed. Once payment is verified and processing begins, cancellation may not be possible. Contact us immediately at +2348080868957 if you need to make changes.
What is your refund policy?
+
We accept refund requests for damaged or incorrect items upon delivery. You must notify us within 24 hours of receiving your order with photographic evidence. Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days back to your original payment method.
Do you deliver outside Nigeria?
+
Currently, we only deliver within Nigeria. We cover all 36 states plus the FCT. International export enquiries can be directed to nerogabfarm@gmail.com.
Are prices negotiable for bulk orders?
+
Yes, bulk orders of 5 bags or more qualify for discounted pricing. Orders above 1 tonne receive priority scheduling and dedicated account management. Contact us directly to discuss your requirements and get a custom quote.
How do I become a supplier?
+
If you are a farmer or agribusiness interested in supplying to Nerogab Farms, fill out the Supplier Expression of Interest form on our homepage. We welcome partnerships with farms across Nigeria, particularly in major agricultural belt areas.
Is Nerogab Farms a registered business?
+
Yes. Nerogab Farms is operated by Nerogab Global Services, a company registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria under RC Number 8148187. Our registered address is 9 Igbinovia Street, Off Odiase, Ikpoba Hill, Ikpoba Okha, Benin, Edo State.
